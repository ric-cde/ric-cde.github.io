What is allergic rhinitis?

A faulty immune response to foreign particles which causes inflammation (blocked nose), excess mucous, irritation, and sinus pain. Two main types exist: seasonal and non-seasonal.

How do I know if I have it?

If your symptoms are limited to summer months, it's likely you have a seasonal allergy (hayfever) to grass or other pollen. If not, you may be allergic to dust mites or animal dander. The best way to be sure is an allergy test (skin prick or blood).

Who is this site for?

While some the tips are specific to allergy sufferers, anyone with a blocked nose may find these remedies useful. Especially for conditions such as sinusitis which tend to have non-allergic causes.
